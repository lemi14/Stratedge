Who Are We?

Strat Edge is a creative and educational hub built by Group 5 of 9-2, who are passionate about combining learning with fun. Our goal is to make education more interactive and engaging by using platforms like Roblox to create game-based experiences that spark curiosity and encourage exploration.

What We Are

We focus on content that blends gaming, teamwork, and learning, offering a fresh perspective on how students can absorb knowledge in today's digital age. Whether it's through custom-built games, collaborative projects, or concept-based challenges, Strat Edge aims to push the boundaries of traditional learning by turning it into an exciting journey.

Why Do We Make These Videos?

We believe that learning doesn't have to be boring—and with the right tools and imagination, it can be something you look forward to. Strat Edge isn’t just a project; it’s a movement toward fun, interactive education for the next generation.
